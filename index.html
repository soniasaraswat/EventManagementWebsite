What We Are

They say, unicorns are a myth, that they don't exist. But in our universe, we fantasize your wildest imaginations. We say, imagine harder, we are ready to show you that nothing is impossible. Giving experiences like no other, creating memories, appreciating the beauty that resides inside a moment, we are EventOgasm, giving you the adventures of a lifetime. Established in the month of July 2017, by two extremely ambitious individuals, Ainesh Jain and Robin, our fairly new family has already taken it to the skies. What drives us is that just like the sky feels alive with the radiance of the sun, that is exactly how a tiny moment can serve as eternal bliss and adorn the journey of life. Such moments, being the essence of the foundation of EventOgasm, are extremely significant to our founders. We have pushed ourselves across all horizons, opening doors to newer and far better worlds. You dream your world, we will build it.
